Main Bar

In our main bar you can enjoy from an extensive range of beverages and if you're hungry order either from our light bytes menu or the full restaurant menu. There's big screens so you can spectate in the major sporting events too!

Restaurant

In the more tranquil setting of our restaurant, you'll be able to savour great food from our east meets west menu, which covers a wide range of tastes, from burger and chips to sushi platters, you'll bound to find something you'll enjoy.

Coding Bar

Our specially designed coding bar offers a quiet space where the background music has been selected to not be distracting. There are free power sockets, USB chargers and LAN connections at every table, and service buttons for when you need more refreshment. You can order any beverages from the main bar, or stick to our wide selection of coffees, teas, and energy drinks directly available in the coding bar. There is also a range of light bytes and snacks should your energy levels become low, or if you'd prefer something more substantial from either the main bar or restaurant, there are charging station laptop lockers available free of charge. As you'd expect there's fast free WIFI too.

Pool Bar

For those who enjoy a bit of active relaxation we have a four-table pool bar where the full drinks menu is available along with a range of light bytes. Be sure to book a table at the main bar on busy nights and the weekend!

Special Events

We're a social bunch, so we like to hold an event or two, whether it be a live band, DJ session, quiz night or even the odd gaming tournament! The schedule can be a bit thick and fast at times so head over to the events page for more details.
